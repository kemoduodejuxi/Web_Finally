STEP 1: Create Daisy

Holding Your Rubik’s Cube

To begin with, holding your Rubik’s Cube with the YELLOW CENTER piece on the UP face.

Action 1

Look at the top layer, Leave the EDGE pieces that have a WHITE tile.

See example below, it is fine if the WHITE tile is not touching the YELLOW CENTER piece at this stage.





Action 2

Look at the middle layer.

Following the algorithms " F' " or " R "

Move EDGE pieces that have a WHITE tile from the MIDDLE layer into the top layer.




Action 3

Look at the bottom layer.

Following the algorithm " F2 "

Move EDGE pieces that have a WHITE tile from the BOTTOM layer into the top layer.






Step finished!

When your Rubik’s Cube has a daisy that looks like below, you can move to Step 2!
