Holding Your Rubik’s Cube

Begin by holding your Rubik’s Cube with the daisy on the UP face.

Look at the FRONT face of the Rubik’s Cube.

Action 1




If your cube is same as above,

follow the algorithm below until the FRONT tile of the UP edge piece matches the CENTER tile color.

Algorithm: " U " OR " U' "

If your cube is same as below, go to Action 2.







Action 2

Follow the algorithm " F2 "

Turn the FRONT face two times so that the WHITE tile is now on the DOWN face like below.






Action 3

Repeat Action 1 & 2 for each WHITE edge.




Action 4

Once all 4 edges have been correctly placed, flip your Rubik’s Cube over to see the WHITE cross

(with matching edge tiles on the RED, BLUE, ORANGE and GREEN faces).





Step finished!
