STEP 4 - Place Edges in Middle

Holding Your Rubik’s Cube

If your cube can match the cube or corresponding colors below,

move to "Action 1: Moving the Edge Piece to the Left" section.













The GREEN pieces in the FRONT face can be replaced to RED, ORANGE or BLUE colors.

The RED pieces in the TOP and LEFT faces can be replaced to BLUE, GREEN or ORANGE colors accordingly.









If your cube can match the cube or corresponding colors below,

move to "Action 1: Moving the Edge Piece to the Right" section.




The GREEN pieces in the FRONT face can be replaced to RED, ORANGE or BLUE colors.

The RED pieces in the TOP and LEFT faces can be replaced to GREEN, BLUE or RED colors accordingly.





Action 1: Moving the Edge Piece to the Left

Follow the first algorithm:

U' L' U L













This algorithm places the edge piece next to its correct corner piece.



Follow the second algorithm:

U F U' F'













This algorithm brings the edge piece to the correct position in the middle layer.



OR



Action 1: Moving the Edge Piece to the Right

Follow the first algorithm:

U R U' R'







This algorithm places the edge piece next to its correct corner piece.



Follow the second algorithm:

U' F' U F







This algorithm brings the edge piece to the correct position in the middle layer.



Action 2

Repeat previous Action 1 until all MIDDLE layer pieces are in the correct positions.





Step finished!

Hover your mouse over the cube below to see another side of the cube.

When the two bottom layers of your Rubik’s Cube look like below,

you can move to solving the third layer. You are two-thirds of the way done!
