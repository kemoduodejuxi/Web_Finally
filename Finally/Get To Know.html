FACES

There are 6 faces on a Rubik’s Cube.






CENTERS

Center pieces have one colored piece which fixed to the internal core.

There are 6 center pieces in a Rubik's Cube.

When correctly solved, each face will be the color of its center piece.



Center piece colors are always opposite each other:

BLUE opposite GREEN




ORANGE opposite RED




WHITE opposite YELLOW







EDGES

There are 12 edge in a Rubik's Cube.






CORNERS

Corner have three colored pieces. There are 8 corner pieces in a Rubik's Cube.







Face Key

Each face is represented by a letter.

U = UP FACE







D = DOWN FACE



















L = LEFT FACE















R = RIGHT FACE







F = FRONT FACE







B = BACK FACE

Each of the move above is a ¼ TURN.


An ALGORITHM is a sequence of moves that you need to follow in a specific order.


When following the algorithms in this guide,

it is important to maintain the FRONT face of your Rubik’s Cube through all of the turns.


If there is a 2 next to the algorithm letter, follow the algorithm 2 times.


A turn is clockwise when looking at that face directly.


If there is an apostrophe (') after the algorithm letter, it means to make an anticlockwise turn of the face.
