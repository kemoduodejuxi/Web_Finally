STEP 3: Solve the White Corners

Holding Your Rubik’s Cube

Begin by holding your Rubik’s Cube with the WHITE cross on the UP face.





Action 1

Locate a corner piece with a WHITE tile in the bottom layer.

If your corner piece fulfills one of these positions, go to Action 2.



OR



OR





















Action 2




The cube above is incorrect.

Rotate the DOWN face until the corner is between the two matching colored center tiles likes one of the cubes below.



OR



OR





















Action 3

Keeping the white cross on the UP face, hold your Rubik’s Cube so the WHITE tile is on the FRONT face.











If the WHITE tile is on the LEFT, likes the example above.

Follow the algorithm:

D L D' L'

Notice: D moves the corner piece out of the way, L brings its corner position down,

D’ moves it into place and then L’ brings it up to the top layer.

OR





If the WHITE tile is on the RIGHT, likes the example above.

Follow the algorithm:

D' R' D R

Notice: D’ moves the corner piece out of the way, R’ brings its corner position down,

D moves it into place and then R brings it up to the top layer.



Action 4

Repeat previous Actions 1 - 3 until all white corner pieces are in the correct positions.




Step finished!

Hover your mouse over the cube below to see another side of the cube.

When all sides of your cube are same as below,

you have one-third solved and you can now learn to solve the middle layer!
