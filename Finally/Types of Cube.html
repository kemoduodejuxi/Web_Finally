Types of Rubik's Cube


Four Axis Cube

This kind of Rubik's cube has four equivalent central axes, among which diamond Rubik's cube and pyramid Rubik's cube are more common.



















Six Axis Cube

This kind of magic cube has six equivalent central axes,

among which n is the most common one × n × N's cube, but the shape does not necessarily make a cube.



















Eight Axis Cube

This kind of cube has eight equivalent central axes, among which Dino cube, platypus and rainbow cube are more common.



















Twelve axis cube

This kind of Rubik's cube has twelve equivalent central axes,

among which the more common ones are five Rubik's cube, Alexander's star, Pyraminx crystal and heliopter cube.
