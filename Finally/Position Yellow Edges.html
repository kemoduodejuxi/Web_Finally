STEP 8: Position the Yellow Edges

Holding Your Rubik's Cube

Hold your Rubik’s Cube so the one face that is a solid color is the BACK, and the Yellow face is the UP face.

It doesn’t matter which face is in the back, if no face is a solid color.

Below is an example of the cube:

(Hover your mouse over the cube below to see the whole cube)





Action 1

F2 U L R' F2 L' R U F2

Follow the algorithm above up to 3 times to move the unsolved edges clockwise or to the left.

When you repeat the algorithm, make sure a solved face is placed at the BACK face of the Rubik’s Cube.

Step finished!
